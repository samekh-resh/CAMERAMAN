# CAMERAMAN
WORKING SCRIPT FOR QUASICAMP/COMEDY HORROR MOVIE


Cameraman is a quasicamp, horror movie about a man, with a head for a camera who has one goal: to get you to take the perfect selfie. And if it isn't... YOU DIE... or that's how the story goes. 


Allegedly, something was reawakened during the times of covid. Something that was deeply buried. But he got brough out, when the world's dependence on camera's deepend during the world quarantine, when peopel put theri hearts and souls and died close to the camera. He came alive. 

CameraMan.


CameraMan follows Sonya, a luddite, radical hacker who has since retired and simply works it at a simply office, as she muddles her day through life. She gets up. Goes to work. Goes home. Eats at the italian resaurant sometimes. But all that changes when her coworker, Janet, shows her the newest internet creepypasta her son showed her, The CameraMAn.
